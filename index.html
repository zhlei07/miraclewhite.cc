Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Standard MOQ is 50 units for most peptide products and 100 units for antioxidant compounds. For first-time orders, we offer a sample pack of 10-20 units to test quality before committing to larger quantities.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank wire transfer (T/T), Western Union, and PayPal for orders under $2,000. For larger orders, we offer 30% deposit with balance before shipment. Alibaba Trade Assurance is also available for added buyer protection.

How long does shipping take?

Express shipping (DHL/FedEx/UPS) takes 5-7 business days to most countries. Economy shipping takes 10-15 business days. Stock items are dispatched within 24-48 hours of payment confirmation.

Do you provide COA (Certificate of Analysis)?

Yes. Every batch comes with a third-party COA showing purity, sterility, and endotoxin testing results. We can also provide GMP certificates and other compliance documents upon request.

Can you do OEM / private label?

Absolutely. We offer full OEM services including custom formulation, packaging design, labeling, and branding. MOQ for OEM is typically 200-500 units depending on the product. Turnaround time is 2-3 weeks.
